# Research Source Register

This document tracks all research sources used in the humanoid robotics book.

## Source Tracking Template

For each research source, record the following information:

| ID | DOI/Link | Title | Summary | Relevance | Validation Status | Date Added |
|----|----------|-------|---------|-----------|-------------------|------------|
| R001 | [DOI/URL] | [Source Title] | [Brief summary of content] | [Why this source is relevant] | [Validated/Not Validated] | [YYYY-MM-DD] |
| R002 | [DOI/URL] | [Source Title] | [Brief summary of content] | [Why this source is relevant] | [Validated/Not Validated] | [YYYY-MM-DD] |

## Research Workflow

1. Research item found
2. Evaluate relevance to book content
3. Tag with source ID
4. Place in research/source-register.md with DOI, link, summary, and relevance note

## Citation Standards

All sources must follow APA citation format and be validated for accuracy.
